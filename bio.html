Toaller Lawson's Biography

Chef Toaller Lawson started off his career as a waiter, gained some experience assisting cooks at events, subsequently becoming a cook at the Movenpick Ambassador Hotel-Accra.

Trained under world renowned chefs such as Chef Lars Spanberg, (then, an Executive Chef of Movenpick Ambassador Hotel), Chef Tahir, Chef Rita, Chef Ali and a host of other many talents.

Chef Lawson still a cook back then had many wonderful opportunities cooking along cooks and chefs in other establishments in Accra rising through the challenges and experiences to becoming an excusive chef.

Chef Lawson is currently working as a private chef in Accra, pouring out love and experiences on cuisines for all intimate and private dinings.
